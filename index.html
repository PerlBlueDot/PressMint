PressMint_

A native Debian WordPress stack.

STATUS: Closed Beta (RC42)
Development in progress.

A modern, high-performance Perl-based installer designed to deploy a hardened WordPress stack on Debian 12/13 servers. It aims to obsolete proprietary stacks by offering a transparent, auditable, and highly optimized "return to basics." It acts as a "scalpel," transforming a fresh VM into a production-ready environment in minutes.
